Prime Mover

In an age of information overload, young people's willpower is easily worn down, making it increasingly difficult to focus on completing tasks. Rather than listening to their parents, today's youth are more inclined to heed their friends' advice. Therefore, we have decided to harness the social power among friends to provide motivation for young people, helping them break free from the vicious cycle of procrastination.


Our application allows users to input tasks or challenges they want to complete into the platform. Users can create their own tasks or record essential things that need to be done. When the set time arrives, the system automatically notifies the user's friends, leveraging peer encouragement or reminders. Ultimately, this approach enables users to autonomously complete the tasks they have set for themselves.
